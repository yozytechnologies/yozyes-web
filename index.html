YOZY Enterprise Suite

The problem statement!

  • No single system to manage the company operations end to end;
  • Need to acquire multiple products & licenses;
  • Difficulty in managing multiple vendors and coordinating product level issues;
  • May lead to higher cost due to acquisition of multiple products;
  • Collaborating & connecting multiple departments using multiple systems;
  • Difficulties in consolidating information at one platform/in a single system;
  • Employee productivity loss due to switching multiple applications for their day to day activities;
  • Integration of multiple systems becomes overhead (for example: SSO, Email).

Key Advantages

  • Principally “One Enterprise One Platform” kept in mind to automate the process end-to-end;
  • Single unified console improves productivity of employees (cut of multiple logins and switch between consoles);
  • Easy to consolidate & collate multiple departments & data under a single platform (avoid data duplication, integrity issues);
  • Cost effective due to cut in license / infra cost of managing multiple systems;
  • Enhanced business workflow and niche UI features;
  • Customizable based on enterprise requirements;
  • Production ready: Secure & scalable architecture;
  • Single point of contact reduces multiple hops in case of dealing platform level issues (no back & forth discussion between multiple teams);
  • Supports bulk data upload and data migration from legacy platform.
